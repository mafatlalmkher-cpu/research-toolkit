Tools and field notes for researchers

A small, free toolkit for the everyday research workflow, from building a literature search to working at the bench, with short guides on how to use each tool well. Every tool is a single web page: no account, no API key, no server, nothing to install. Use it online or download it and open it any time, even offline.

Why this exists. Good research software is often locked behind subscriptions, logins, or institutional access that many scientists, especially in low-income settings, do not have. Everything here is free, opens from a single file, keeps your data on your own machine, and is released under the MIT licence so anyone can copy, host, translate, or adapt it. Nothing is sold, and nothing watches what you do.
