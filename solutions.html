Solution & dilution calculator

The four conversions that turn up whenever you make a buffer or a working stock. Pick a tab, type what you know, and leave the unknown blank.

Dilute a stock

Leave exactly one box empty and it is solved from the other three.

×
×
–

Concentration units must be compatible (all molar, or all the same mass/volume). The recipe assumes you add diluent to the stock to reach the final volume.

Molarity, mass & volume

Enter the molecular weight, then leave one of concentration / volume / mass blank.

–

mass = concentration × volume × molecular weight. Leave concentration, volume, or mass blank to solve for it.

Percent solution

How much solute for a target percentage and final volume.

–

w/v expresses grams of solute per 100 mL of solution; v/v is mL per 100 mL; w/w is grams per 100 g (density converts the final volume to mass).

Serial dilution planner

Lay out a dilution series with equal volume in each tube.

Each tube is brought to the same total volume. Transfer the shown volume from the previous tube into fresh diluent, mix, then carry forward. Prepare a little extra in the first tube to cover the transfers.
