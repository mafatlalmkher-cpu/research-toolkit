What it does

The PRISMA 2020 flow diagram shows how many records you found, removed, screened, and included in a systematic review. This tool draws it from the numbers you type, computes the downstream totals so they always reconcile, and exports the figure as SVG (to edit later) or PNG (to drop straight into a manuscript).

How to use it

  1. Open the tool.
  2. Type the records found in databases and registers, and how many duplicates you removed.
  3. Enter the records excluded at screening, the reports not retrieved, and the full-text exclusions with their reasons.
  4. Watch the diagram update live. If a computed total turns red, a number upstream needs fixing.
  5. Download the diagram as SVG or PNG.

Good to know

Runs entirely offline. Switch on the optional second arm if you also found studies through citation searching, websites, or contacts. Reference: Page MJ et al., BMJ 2021;372:n71.
