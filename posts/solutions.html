What it does

Four bench conversions in one page: C₁V₁ = C₂V₂ dilutions, molarity from mass, percent solutions, and a serial-dilution planner that lays out every transfer volume. It is unit-aware across M, mM, µM, nM and L, mL, µL.

How to use it

  1. Pick a tab.
  2. Type what you know and leave the unknown box blank.
  3. Read the result and the suggested recipe.
  4. For a series, set the starting concentration, the fold per step, the number of steps, and the volume per tube.

Good to know

Runs offline. Double-check critical recipes; rounding is for display only.
