What it does

A set of everyday DNA and RNA calculators: sequence analysis (reverse complement, percent GC, melting temperature, and translation), A260 nucleic-acid quantification, mass to moles to copies conversions, and oligonucleotide molecular weight.

How to use it

  1. Open the tool and choose a tab.
  2. Paste a sequence to analyse, or type your A260 reading, fragment length, or oligo sequence.
  3. Read the results, which update as you type.
  4. Copy any output you need.

Good to know

Fully offline, so it works with confidential sequences and on a poor connection. Melting temperatures are quick estimates; for primer design, confirm with a nearest-neighbour calculation as well.
