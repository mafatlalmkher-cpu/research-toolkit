What it does

The fiddly part of making a medium is the hormones. This tool works out how much stock to add for a target concentration, converts mg·L⁻¹ to µM for the common plant growth regulators (BA, IBA, NAA, TDZ, 2,4-D, and others), helps you prepare a stock with the right solvent, and scales a recipe to any volume.

How to use it

  1. Pick the regulator; its molecular weight loads automatically.
  2. Enter the target concentration, the batch volume, and your stock strength.
  3. Read how much stock to pipette.
  4. Use the other tabs to convert units, prepare a stock, or scale a recipe.

Good to know

Runs offline. Molecular weights are standard values; confirm against your reagent's certificate of analysis. The solvent and sterilisation notes are general guidance, not a substitute for the product data sheet.
