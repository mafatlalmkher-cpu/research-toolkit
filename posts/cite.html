What it does

Drop in a list of identifiers and the tool fetches each record from Crossref or PubMed and formats it in APA, Vancouver, or Harvard, alongside BibTeX and RIS you can import into any reference manager.

How to use it

  1. Paste DOIs or PubMed IDs, one per line.
  2. Click Fetch and format.
  3. Switch between styles with the tabs.
  4. Copy all of them, or take the BibTeX or RIS to import elsewhere.

Good to know

This tool needs an internet connection for the lookups. Crossref and PubMed are free and need no key. Citation styles vary between journals, so check author limits and punctuation against your target before submitting.
