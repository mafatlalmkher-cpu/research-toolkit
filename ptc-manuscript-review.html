Forensic checklist for tissue-culture manuscripts

What a plant tissue culture paper needs before it should be accepted: the checks an honest author runs before submitting, and the ones a careful reviewer and editor run before recommending acceptance. It covers micropropagation, regeneration, somatic embryogenesis, and transformation studies. Tick through it, add notes, and export a summary.
